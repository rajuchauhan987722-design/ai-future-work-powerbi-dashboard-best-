# ai-future-work-powerbi-dashboard-best-
Power BI dashboard analyzing global AI adoption, job risk, and future workforce trends with interactive insights and data-driven storytelling.
🚀 AI Skills & Future Work – Power BI Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project is part of the DataBuzz Power BI Challenge, focusing on analyzing the global impact of Artificial Intelligence on jobs, skills, and industries.

The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of AI adoption, job risk, salary trends, and investment patterns across different regions and industries.

📊 Key Insights
💰 $975M+ AI Investment analyzed across years
🌍 57% Average AI Adoption Rate globally
👨‍💼 3 Billion Jobs Impacted (created vs at risk)
⚠️ High automation risk observed in roles like Data Entry & Telemarketing
📈 Technology sector leads in AI adoption (~60%)
